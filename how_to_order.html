HOW TO ORDER WITH US

Ordering from us is easy and straightforward. To get started, simply visit our Home Page and browse through the different products we have on offer. Once you have found something you like, proceed to the contact page on our website.
On the contact page, fill in your details, including your name, email address, and phone number. In the message section, indicate the product you wish to purchase, its quantity, and any other relevant information you would like us to know. We have a policy that requires a 50% downpayment, which can be sent through GCash. After making the payment, take a screenshot of the receipt and attach it to your message. You can also contact us through our sending email or our Facebook page and attach the screenshot there.
Once we receive your message and downpayment, we will confirm your order and provide you with an estimated delivery date. We will also keep you updated on the progress of your order, so you always know what is happening. In case you have any questions or concerns, feel free to reach out to us through our Facebook page or email, and we will be happy to assist you. We value our customers and strive to provide the best possible service, so you can shop with confidence knowing that you are in good hands.

ABOUT US

Welcome to Rita's Delight, where we create delicious and high-quality desserts and savory dishes made with fresh ingredients. Located in Consolacion, Cebu, we specialize in classic Filipino desserts like leche flan and mango float, as well as savory dishes such as chicken schnitzel and chicken cordon bleu. All of our dishes are made to order and we take pride in exceeding our customers' expectations. Thank you for choosing Rita's Delight and we look forward to serving you soon!
